# Trust & Review

**URL:** `/trust-review`

## Purpose

Give buyers one public page that ties together the CCAI trust story: screening signals, human review, data freshness, PI boundaries, and examination readiness.

## Audience

- Compliance officers evaluating whether CCAI fits their operating model
- Security and procurement reviewers checking reliance boundaries
- Counsel or audit stakeholders who need clear language about what CCAI does and does not decide

## Positioning

CCAI is a system for producing and organizing screening evidence. It supports customer review workflows, but it does not replace customer judgment or become the final compliance authority.

## Page Sections

1. Hero: the review boundary is clear
2. Four pillars: screening evidence, human review quality, PI assistance boundary, exam narrative
3. Operating model: signal, context, review, explain
4. Boundaries: evaluation output, data freshness, customer judgment, agentic operations
5. CTA: continue into data freshness and human review

## Claim Discipline

- Say "screening signal" instead of "final compliance decision"
- Say "supports a defensible process" instead of "ensures compliance"
- Say "PI assists" instead of "PI decides"
- Say "freshness context is part of reliance" instead of promising source completeness
