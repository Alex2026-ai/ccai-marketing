## Section: FAQ

**Q: Can I start without a sales call?**
A: Yes. Starter and Professional are fully self-serve. Create a tenant, generate an API key, and start screening immediately.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my monthly entity limit?**
A: You'll receive a notification. Screening continues — we don't cut you off mid-batch. Overage pricing applies at the per-entity rate for your tier.

**Q: Can I switch tiers?**
A: Yes, at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes. Try the sandbox with sample data at no cost. No credit card required.

**Q: What's included in the audit trail?**
A: Per-entity decision packages with integrity hashes, full decision paths, enrichment steps, snapshot references, and promotion logic. Available at every tier.
